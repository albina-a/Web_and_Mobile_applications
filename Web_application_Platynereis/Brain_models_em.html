Brain models


Interactive model of the Platynereis neuronal circutry

Drag the mouse to rotate the model and scroll to zoom

Platynereis neuronal connectivity was reconstructed from the electron microscopy images and modeled using the 3D graphics software Blender. The cell bodies of the reconstructed neurons were approximated with the spheres and axons and dendrites with the tube-like structures. Blender file can be downloaded from here.
